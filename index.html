1.

Hosts list a celebration

They choose the event type, date, location area, number of guest seats, contribution amount, and what guests should know.

2.

Guests request to attend

Guests browse listings and send a short request explaining why they'd like to attend.

3.

Hosts approve guests

The host reviews each request and decides who to welcome — no obligation either way.

4.

Guests receive details

Approved guests get etiquette guidance, arrival details, and payment instructions. Online checkout may be added later.

For hosts

Share your celebration on your terms.

List a wedding, holiday gathering, or cultural event. You decide how many guests to welcome, the contribution amount, what guests should know, and whether each request needs your approval before details are shared.

  • Choose guest seats
  • Set contribution amount
  • Approve guests first
  • Share etiquette and boundaries
  • Keep full address private until approval
For guests

Attend with curiosity and respect.

Browse cultural weddings and celebrations, request a seat, and learn what to expect before attending. Guests are expected to follow the host's dress code, photo rules, timing, and cultural guidance.
